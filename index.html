About Me

Hello! I'm Howie Miao, a CS Major in my last semester at CU Boulder. I used to be a Psychology Major when I first started here in 2018, but after a semester I realized that maybe it wasn't for me. I continued to stray away from the family default of a major in Computer Science and attempted a full year in Business. I had completed the basics of Micro and Macroeconics before I realized I seemed to just be avoiding my passion in tech just because my family had done it before, but I had grown up with it my whole life and it was a huge part of who I was. I find a lot of pleasure in creating things from scratch, learning and understanding how every internal works together and creates a functioning product, as well as creating somethign that makes one's day or life easier. That was and still is my biggest drive.

With my last semester I've spent most of my time looking for a job and area to apply my honed skill set towards. With previous projects like python integrated bots through a Raspberry Pi Pico, or touching base on new topics like Machine Learning or PyTorch, I've time and time again found passion in creating new things that I never would've thought could have existed just a handful of years ago.
